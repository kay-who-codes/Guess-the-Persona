How To Play

Goal

The goal is for players to guess the role/occupation/character and secret of the randomly generated persona.

Gameplay

  1. Generate a random persona using the "Generate Persona" button.
  2. One player reads the persona description out loud, but omits the role/occupation/character and secret.
  3. The other players must guess what the persona's role/occupation/character is and what their secret is.
  4. Use the "Reveal Role" and "Reveal Secret" buttons to check if guesses were correct.
  5. Players can decide their own scoring system and number of rounds.

Example Persona

"A Middle-Aged Adult Conspiracy Theorist with a speech impediment who constantly talks about aliens.

Their big secret is: They work for the government agency they constantly rant about."

Players would need to guess: "Conspiracy Theorist" and "Works for the government agency they constantly rant about"
